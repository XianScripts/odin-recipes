Turkey Tacos

Simple, delicious turkey tacos.

These are a sure fire hit! They taste just like regular tacos but healthier! Easy to make, easy to consume! The whole family will love them, and you dont have to worry about excess fat content They're high in protein and they make the perfect post workout meal and dinner that will fill you up!

Ingredients

  • Turkey meat
  • Taco shells
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese
  • Taco seasoning
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook meat and add seasoning after meat is browned 7-9 minutes
  2. Heat shells in oven at 350 degree 5 minutes
  3. Prep lettuce, cheese, sour cream in seperate bowls
  4. When meat is done, add a scoop to taco shell, fill with other Ingredients
  5. Serve and eat
